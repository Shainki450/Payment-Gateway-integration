#Donnation for Amarnath Disaster

A flood is common. Floods and heavy rains have destroyed many homes, and when you find a child standing at your door with fundraising for flood relief letter, you should give wholeheartedly. This letter is used to collect funds for victims of a flood. The funds go towards helping them get stable again. As it is said by all great people, as you sow, so you reap. So helping the flood victims will not only give us satisfaction and heart’s contentment but also will be another good thing in our kitty making us worthy of help when needed. It is rare for people to help another especially when you do not know what is happening to them. With a lot of natural calamities destroying lives and homes, many individuals end up as displaced or even a refugee. It is never late to join hands and do something for your helpless brother or sister and spread happiness all over.
